FriquiWhat?

On June 2019 we launched FriquiFund Barcelona with the mission of supporting budding geeks (friquis) in developing their careers.

Friqui means geek, in Spanish. For us it is a cheeky way to say: “people interested in pursuing careers in STEM”. FriquiFund members are all professionals related to the STEM world, willing to donate time and money to out mission.

Today we are more than 100 members and we have donated over 20,000€ to NGOs supporting young tech. students in need in Barcelona.
